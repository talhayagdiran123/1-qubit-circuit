# Quantum Notes

## Qubit
A qubit can be 0, 1, or both at the same time.

## Superposition
Superposition means a qubit is in multiple states at once.

## Hadamard Gate
The Hadamard gate creates superposition.

## Measurement
Measurement collapses the qubit into 0 or 1.

## Result
The result is probabilistic (random but predictable distribution).
